Being considerate isn't always easy

Every time we interact with another person there is a natural expectation that each person will be considerate of the other. Unfortunately, sometimes - whether by accident or ignorance - people can do or say things that are thoughtless, rude, or may even hurt someone's feelings. It doesn't have to be this way.

Helping people find Harmony

We envision a future in which people can use Project Harmony to be more aware of the emotions of other people. Deployed on a wearable computing device, our application will analyze the emotions of nearby people in any given social situation. On the spot, it will display emotion information to the wearer in real time, as well as be able to offer advice and guidance on how to react to the situation in a considerate way. Armed with this information, the wearer can learn to be more considerate to the people they interact with in everyday situations. In this way, the world can become a better place, one person at a time.
